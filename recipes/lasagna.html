Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked dish traditionally made by layering wide, flat pasta sheets with various fillings like meat sauce (ragù), creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Ragu Bolognese
  • White sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Layering
  4. Bake
  5. Rest
